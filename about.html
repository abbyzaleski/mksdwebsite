About the Studio

Opened in 1998, Melissa Kathleen's School of Dance has been a staple for the arts community in Albemarle, North Carolina over the last 23 years. Beginning with only 17 students, the studio has grown exponentially over the years, but has remained a welcoming environment for dancers of all ages. Melissa Kathleen's School of Dance offers classes in a variety of dance styles, encouraging students to express their creativity through the art of dance, and instilling confidence as they grow.

About the Owner

Melissa Zaleski is the owner and director of Melissa Kathleen’s School of Dance. Melissa began studying dance in 1973 at the age of two in Jacksonville, Florida under the direction of Virginia Dell Hobbs. In 1991, she qualified and became a member of Dance Masters of America, an international organization of teachers certified to teach by test. She became a member of Carolina in 1998 and is currently serving on their Board of Directors as Vice President. Melissa is active in her local community in supporting the arts and has performed in, directed and choreographed for the local Stanly County Dancing with the Stars for many years, as well as working with the Uwharrie Players on several summer productions. Many of her current and former students have been active with the children’s theater group, The Talent Company. Melissa is dedicated to continuing her own dance education as well and encouraging her students by taking master classes from different artists throughout the years. to name a few. A lifelong passion for sharing the art of dance led Melissa to open Melissa Kathleen’s School of Dance in 1998. Since then she has enjoyed teaching many wonderful young people and adults in Albemarle. Melissa has been married to her husband, D.J., since 1992 and they have two children, Abby and Zach.
